# Starbucks Consumer Behavior Analysis (SPSS)

Analyzing the influence of cultural dynamics, product adaptation, and brand perception on customer experience and purchase intention.

## Overview

This project explores how consumer behavior, cultural factors, and Starbucks’ branding strategies impact customer experience and purchase intentions across multiple states in India. Using SPSS, hypotheses were tested to uncover insights for market entry strategy, brand positioning, and promotional strategies.

## Key Skills & Tools

- SPSS: Hypothesis testing, regression analysis, ANOVA, Chi-square tests
- Data Analysis: Formulating hypotheses, interpreting statistical outputs, deriving actionable insights
- Business Insights: Market entry strategy, brand positioning, and cultural adaptation

## Hypotheses Tested

1. Cultural influence on consumer behavior
2. Cultural impact has a non-significant effect on overall customer experience
3. Maharashtra shows high adaptability to cultural dynamics, suggesting pricing strategies could be optimized
4. Brand competitive advantage through cultural adaptation
5. Cultural sensitivity improves brand recognition, with Delhi showing the strongest competitive edge
6. Product adaptation & localization impact on global brand image
7. Integration of cultural norms positively affects global brand identity
8. Delhi demonstrates the strongest brand image, Telangana the weakest
9. Premium brand image & pricing influence on purchase intention
10. Starbucks’ premium perception, discount, and loyalty programs significantly drive consumer purchase intentions

## Key Insights

- Market entry strategy strongly influences customer experience
- Positive cultural adaptation enhances competitive advantage and brand recognition
- Product localization supports global brand identity
- Premium pricing and loyalty programs are critical in influencing purchase behavior

## Recommendations

1. Refine pricing strategies and menu offerings based on regional consumer preferences
2. Focus on improving customer experience to strengthen global brand image
3. Enhance discount and loyalty programs to increase purchase intention
